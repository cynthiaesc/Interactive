cynthia Escalante

about + contact

Cynthia Escalante is a multidisciplinary designer, her work covers a wide range of areas in design, including urbanism, architecture, graphic identity, posters, books and more. Escalante was born in El Paso, TX and raised in Ciudad Juarez, CHIH. In 2016 she received her Bachelor of Science in Architecture from Texas Tech University College of Architecture in El Paso. After graduation, she worked as a landscape designer in El Paso, TX and later as a City Planner at the City of Sunland Park. She is currently studying to obtain her MFA in Design/Graphic Design at California College of the Arts in San Francisco, CA.
